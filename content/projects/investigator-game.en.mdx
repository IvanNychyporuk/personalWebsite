## Defining the Objective: Atmosphere & Scope

<img src="/projects/kicosp/slide-en-02.jpg" alt="Defining the Objective — Atmosphere and Scope" />

- **Core Objective:** Build a functional Unity prototype centered on investigative mechanics and puzzle gameplay.
- **Genre Focus:** A classic 2D point-and-click adventure prioritizing atmosphere and immediate scene readability.
- **Scope:** A focused build featuring two distinct, fully playable locations.

## The Precedent: Genre & Inspiration

<img src="/projects/kicosp/slide-en-03.jpg" alt="The Precedent — Genre and Inspiration" />

- **Lineage:** Inspired by the narrative depth of the *Black Mirror Trilogy*, *Broken Sword*, and *Monkey Island*.
- **Mechanics:** The point-and-click format is selected to optimally support investigative gameplay and logic puzzles.
- **Aesthetic Utility:** 2D assets honor genre roots while ensuring high production efficiency and visual clarity.

## The Equipment: Modern Production Stack

<img src="/projects/kicosp/slide-en-04.jpg" alt="The Equipment — Modern Production Stack" />

- **Concept & Writing:** ChatGPT generates story structures and investigation logic; Nano Banana establishes consistent character styles.
- **Asset Creation:** Midjourney creates detailed environmental backgrounds; Ludo AI produces sprite sheets for movement.
- **Assembly:** Unity acts as the primary engine, utilizing Adventure Creator to manage hotspots, inventory, and branching mechanics.

## The Subjects: Protagonist & NPCs

<img src="/projects/kicosp/slide-en-05.jpg" alt="The Subjects — Protagonist and NPCs" />

- **The Investigator:** A skeptical federal agent acting as a "reader of reality," solving ritualistic mysteries via logic.
- **The Gatekeepers:** Characters like Inspector Mara Voss and the Duty Officer provide procedural friction and control evidence access.
- **The Witnesses:** Locals like Anika provide crucial leads and ground the "small-town uncanny" atmosphere.
- **Mechanic:** Branching dialogue navigates bureaucratic obstacles to reveal contradictions.

## The Method: Visual & Animation Pipeline

<img src="/projects/kicosp/slide-en-06.jpg" alt="The Method — Visual and Animation Pipeline" />

- **Generation:** Consistent character visuals and turnaround references are established via Nano Banana.
- **Processing:** Raw images are processed through Ludo AI's "sprite animation" tool to generate full movement sheets.
- **Integration:** Unity imports these sheets to create functional animation clips.
- **Implementation:** Adventure Creator applies clips to character controllers for navigating walkable areas and hotspots.

## The Assembly: Unity Integration & Result

<img src="/projects/kicosp/slide-en-07.jpg" alt="The Assembly — Unity Integration and Result" />

- **The Framework:** Adventure Creator assembles backgrounds, sprites, and logic like LEGO blocks.
- **The Build:** Two functional scenes (Police Station and Diner) populated with interactive evidence and NPCs.
- **The Loop:** A complete investigative cycle featuring UV light puzzles, inventory gating, and a case journal UI.
- **The Result:** A playable prototype concluding with a supernatural twist that validates the core mystery.
